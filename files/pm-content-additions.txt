## What is Agile?

A philosophy of software development that values:
- **Individuals and interactions** over processes and tools

**Scrum** is the most popular Agile methodology.

**Product Owner (That's You!)**
- Defines the vision
- Prioritizes backlog
- Accepts/rejects work

**Scrum Master**
- Facilitates ceremonies

- Engineers, designers, QA

- Cross-functional

**1. Product Backlog**
- Prioritized list of all work

**2. Sprint Backlog**
- Work committed for current sprint
- Team owns execution

**3. Increment**
- Potentially shippable product at end of sprint

**Sprint Planning** (2-4 hours)
- What can we deliver this sprint?
- How will we do it?

- Demo what was built

- PM decides: ship or iterate?

**Sprint Retrospective** (1 hour)
- What went well?
- What could improve?
- Action items for next sprint
- Team-only (PM usually not present)

- Break down stories
- Add details
- Estimate effort
